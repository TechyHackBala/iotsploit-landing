Dahua DVR RPC Vulnerability
On 7 March 2017 an anonymous researcher Bashis published details of vulnerabilities discovered in Dahua manufactured video cameras (or CCTV cameras). According to Shodan, there is an estimated 400,000 IP addresses that use Dahua equipment. The vulnerability allows an unauthorised user to gain admin privileges to Dahua kit.
Our Mission is to enable researchers eliminate vulnerabilities in their cameras and to equip them with tools and methods to update or disable affected devices.
